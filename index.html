FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about LIC policies, premium payments, claims, and more.

What is the minimum age to buy LIC policy?
The minimum age to purchase a LIC policy varies by plan. Generally, it ranges from 8 years to 18 years. For most plans, the minimum age is 18 years for self-purchase.
How much premium should I pay for adequate coverage?
A general rule is to have life insurance coverage of 10-15 times your annual income. The premium depends on your age, health, and chosen plan. We recommend 10-15% of your annual income for insurance.
What documents are required for policy purchase?
You need age proof (Aadhar/Passport/Birth Certificate), identity proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport), address proof, income proof (salary slips/ITR), and medical reports if required.
How long does claim settlement take?
LIC has an excellent claim settlement record. Most claims are settled within 30 days of receiving complete documentation. The process is transparent and customer-friendly.
Can I pay premium online?
Yes, LIC offers multiple online payment options including net banking, credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets through their official website and mobile app.
